Success story of CSE department students programming contest

In this year CSE department’s team BerthoCoder attended Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET) NCPC Preliminary Contest 2017. Our team solved 4 problems out of 7 problems and stand 198th position out of 1066 teams. In addition, all members of BerthoCoder are new in Competitive Programming. This is a rare achievement of CSE stamfordians.

Economics Quiz Competition 2016

The Department of Economics, Stamford University Bangladesh has accomplished the first round of “Economics Quiz Competition 2016”. The Chairman of the department, Prof. Dr. Md. Habibur Rahman, has inaugurated the quiz competition. The undergraduate and graduate students of the department have participated in the competition. The competition is organized to furnish the basic knowledge of economics among students.

10th ULAB Fair Play Cup T20 Cricket Tournament 2017

The cricket Team of Stamford University Bangladesh, is participating in the “10th ULAB Fair Play Cup T20 Cricket Tournament 2017”. In the group round, Stamford Cricket Team recorded a comprehensive victory against University of Asia Pacific (UAP) by 53 runs. In the second match, they beat North South University by 9 runs. Stamford Cricket team has qualified for SUPER EIGHT stage as a group champion. In both matches, Mr. Saddam Hossain of Stamford Cricket team was selected as the man of the match for his outstanding performance. Mr. Rabiul Kabir (Manager) and Mr. Jaynal Abedin (Chief Adviser) of Stamford Cricket team were present in the prize giving ceremony.
